About Us

Casa Barista & Co. is not a regular coffee shop. As is evident around the world, there are endless options when it comes to coffee, especially in industrialized corporations. Casa Barista is the complete opposite. This coffee would be what is called Specialty Coffee, or "Specialty Coffee".

Our Story

We want to break two paradigms. No. 1, Coffee doesn't just taste like "coffee." Like any other fruit, you will get a special cup if it is worked from the origin, processed, and served with passion and quality controls. No. 2, If we modify ingredients and work with traditional dishes to make them healthier, what matters is the quality of the calories you consume.
